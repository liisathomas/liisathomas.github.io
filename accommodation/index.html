Accommodation


The best areas to stay in Tallinn are probably Kalamaja (closest to us!), Kadriorg, the city centre (Kesklinn) and Old Town (Vanalinn). The latter is probably the costliest of the four.

You can book a hotel for example here. We ourselves have had good experiences with Airbnb which offers a good selection of rental apartments for less money.
