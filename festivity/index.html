Festivity


Main celebration

We will get married on 6 August with our families present and would love to celebrate with all of you on Saturday, 8 August. The festivity starts at 4 o’clock in the afternoon in BOTIK baar, which is located in an old rubber boots production complex. You can find the place in the Kopli quarter in the Northeast of Tallinn. It is easy to reach by public transport or taxi (see Getting Around). There will be food and drinks and dance and things. Ah, the dress code! There are no particular expectations from our side, but if you really don’t know what to do then think semi-formal or festive. Be advised that August nights may already be a little chilly in Estonia.

Welcome drinks

If you are in Tallinn already on Friday, 7 August, why don’t you join us for some informal welcome drinks? We will meet around 7 o’clock in the evening and have a few drinks at the seaside (weather permitting). We’ll update you on the location and specifics closer to the event.

Wedding gift

The presence of your company is the one gift that really matters. However, if you’d like to spoil us, we will be grateful for monetary contributions towards our new future.
