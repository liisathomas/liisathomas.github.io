Corona


The situation regarding the coronavirus is currently under control in Estonia. Since April there have only been single days with more than 10 positive cases detected despite comparatively frequent testing (see here). Most of the restrictions have been lifted by now and events and gatherings are allowed to take place. Of course, we continue to monitor the situation and will update the information on this site regularly. We also hope that by early August rapid coronavirus tests will be available for everyone to use either here or before travelling.

Timely information on travelling to Estonia can be found here and here.

At the event, we will be relying on the governmental advice to remain conscious of social distancing, wash hands, provide hand sanitizers, and follow the safety guidelines of the establishment. We will also provide masks for everyone, although wearing masks is not compulsory in Estonia. Our main focus is to keep all of us safe and comfortable around each other. In case you reconsider your attendance, please let us know at your earliest convenience. We would certainly miss you very much, but naturally one should stay home when feeling sick.
